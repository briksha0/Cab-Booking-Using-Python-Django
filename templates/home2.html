User-friendly Interface

Simple Design

Intuitive, clean, and visually appealing design to make navigation smooth for all users.

Responsive Layout

Optimized for different devices (desktop, mobile, tablet) ensuring a seamless experience across all screen sizes.

Easy Booking Process

Streamlined steps for booking with minimal clicks (choose location, cab type, confirm booking).

Booking Management

Real-time Availability

Show available cabs in real-time for accurate and quick bookings.

Scheduled Rides

Allow users to schedule rides in advance by setting pickup times and dates.

Ride Later Option

Book a cab for a later time/date, useful for airport transfers or business trips.

Location-based Features

GPS-based Pickup and Drop-off

Automatic location detection using GPS for easier and faster booking.

Live Tracking

Track the driver in real-time after booking, with an estimated arrival time (ETA) for better transparency.

ETA Display

Estimate the arrival time of the driver and the expected duration of the journey.

Secure Payment Options

Multiple Payment Methods

Accept a variety of payment options (credit/debit cards, digital wallets like PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash).

In-App Payments

Enable secure, seamless payments within the app for a smoother experience.

Payment Receipt

Automatically generate and send payment receipts via email or SMS after the ride is completed.

Driver and Vehicle Information

Driver Profile

Display the driver’s name, photo, rating, and vehicle details (license plate, model) to ensure user safety and comfort.

Driver Rating and Feedback

Allow users to rate drivers and provide feedback after each ride, helping maintain service quality.

Driver Contact

Provide the option to contact the driver for coordination or updates prior to the ride.
