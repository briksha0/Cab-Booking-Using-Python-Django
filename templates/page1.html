Agra is home to the Taj Mahal, which is one of the seven wonders of the world. This city in Uttar Pradesh is the top tourist destination in India. It is rich in history with stunning monuments and gardens that reflect the grandeur of the Mughal era. Agra is famous for its vibrant food culture and known for its traditional arts and crafts. This makes it a must-visit destination.

Agra stands on the banks of River Yamuna. Once the capital of the powerful Mughal empire, it proudly showcases a royal heritage that attracts people from around the world.
